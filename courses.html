ENT 302:

Entrepreneurship Studies I

ENT 302 are practical entrepreneurship courses. In these two courses students are exposed to the “Chrisland University Sustainability Project (CLUSP)” which has a focus of connecting entrepreneurship teaching and theory into student generated sustainable development products and ideas that solve critical real life problems.

Ideas generated are related to sustainability projects:

i. Eco-friendly Business (waste recycling, eco-friendly production processes)

ii. Green house cleaning products (using all-natural and eco-friendly cleaning products)

iii. Sustainable construction materials (3D homes)

iv. Technology that enhances service delivery timelines (AI adoption)

v. Renewable plastics Technology adoption for food preservation, food distribution, and to support agricultural output
